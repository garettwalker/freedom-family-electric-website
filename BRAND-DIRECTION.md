# Freedom Family Electric Brand Direction

## Working concept

**Mountain Light Badge**

A muted, mountain-inspired brand system for a Christian, family-led electrical business in King, NC. The design references Pilot Mountain and Hanging Rock without becoming a literal state park logo. The cross is integrated into the mountain line as a quiet symbol of faith, service, and steadiness.

## Brand attributes

- Grounded
- Dependable
- Family-led
- Faith-informed
- Premium residential
- Clean and practical
- Local to the NC foothills

## Typography direction

- Display: Georgia or a future premium serif for warmth, trust, and family feel.
- Body: System sans-serif stack for speed, readability, and professional contractor utility.

## Website positioning

Primary audience:

- Builders of custom homes
- Builders of spec homes
- Homeowners building large residences

Secondary audience:

- Homeowners needing service calls or smaller residential electrical work

## Messaging hierarchy

1. Premium new build residential electrical
2. Reliable builder coordination
3. Clean rough-in and trim-out work
4. Local King, NC and foothills service area
5. Faith and family values expressed through service, not slogans

## Visual direction

- Earth tones, no neon blue or electric yellow
- Mountain and foothill references
- Clean job-site illustrations with open wall wiring and finish-stage electrical work
- No faces in generic project imagery
- Family photo reserved for About page and only after owner approval

## Future logo refinements

- Test a more compact badge for hats and shirts
- Create a one-color version for embroidery
- Create a horizontal lockup for website header and truck decals
- Create a simplified favicon and social media avatar
- Explore whether the cross should stay visible or be reduced further
