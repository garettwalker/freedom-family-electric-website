What we do best

Premium residential electrical for large new homes.

Freedom Family Electric is positioned around the work that requires planning, consistency, and trust: large residential builds where the electrical system must support the way a family will actually live in the home.

That means coordinated rough-in, practical lighting placement, code-conscious installs, clean panels, and attention to the finishing details that show up once the walls are closed.

Rough-in

Open wall wiring

Layout, routing, boxes, circuits, and builder coordination before insulation and sheetrock.

Trim-out

Finish after sheetrock

Switches, outlets, fixtures, lighting details, and final checks that bring the home online.

Service

Selective service calls

Troubleshooting, repairs, updates, and small projects when availability allows.
